About Me

Hi, I’m Aashif Sajah, a dynamic and driven Software Engineering undergraduate from IIT, Sri Lanka. My journey is fueled by an insatiable curiosity for technology and a passion for entrepreneurship. From writing my first line of code to building innovative projects, I’ve always sought to blend creativity with technical precision.

I specialize in full-stack development, mastering tools like Java Spring Boot, Angular, and MySQL. Beyond coding, I thrive in collaborative environments, leading projects and contributing to meaningful solutions that impact lives.

But I’m not just a developer—I’m an innovator with a vision. As the founder of IframesLK, I’ve turned my entrepreneurial aspirations into reality, offering unique, high-quality framed products. With plans to expand into international markets, I’m committed to combining artistry and business to build a globally recognized brand.

Outside of work, I’m a passionate adventurer. Whether it’s hiking, cliff jumping, or planning my next business move, I approach every challenge with energy and determination.

As I continue to grow, my mission is to bridge the gap between technology and real-world impact, creating solutions that inspire and drive change.
